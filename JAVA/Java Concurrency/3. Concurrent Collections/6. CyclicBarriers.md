**Analogy:** Imagine a group of friends hiking different paths to reach a viewpoint (the barrier). Each friend waits at the viewpoint (`await()`) until the *entire group* has arrived. Once the last friend arrives, they all admire the view together (barrier breaks, threads are released) and then decide to hike to the *next* viewpoint (the barrier resets and can be used again).

Think of it like a **meeting point** for a fixed number of threads. Threads arrive at the barrier (`await()`) and wait there *until all* threads in the group have arrived. Once the last thread arrives, the barrier breaks, and *all* waiting threads are released simultaneously. The barrier then resets itself for potential reuse.
