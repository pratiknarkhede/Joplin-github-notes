## **Core system design fundamentals**

### **1\. Foundational Concepts**

1.  **Scalability**
    
    - **Vertical vs. Horizontal Scaling**: Trade-offs between adding resources to a single node vs. distributing load across nodes .
    - **Load Balancing**: Techniques (round-robin, least connections), tools (ELB, HAProxy), and redundancy strategies .
    - **Latency vs. Throughput**: Optimizing for speed (e.g., milliseconds per request) vs. volume (requests per second) .
2.  **Consistency & Availability**
    
    - **CAP Theorem**: Understanding trade-offs between Consistency, Availability, and Partition Tolerance in distributed systems .
    - **Consistency Models**: Strong, eventual, and weak consistency (e.g., databases vs. caching) .
    - **ACID vs. BASE**: Transactional guarantees (ACID) vs. high availability (BASE) .

* * *

### **2\. Architectural Components**

3.  **Database Design**
    
    - **SQL vs. NoSQL**: Use cases for relational (MySQL) vs. distributed (Cassandra, DynamoDB) databases .
    - **Replication & Sharding**: Master-slave replication, partitioning strategies (range, hash-based) .
    - **Indexing & Query Optimization**: B-trees, secondary indexes, and denormalization .
4.  **Caching Strategies**
    
    - **In-Memory Caches**: Redis, Memcached for high-read scenarios .
    - **CDNs**: Offloading static content (e.g., CloudFront, Akamai) .
    - **Cache Eviction Policies**: LRU, FIFO, and TTL-based strategies .

* * *

### **3\. Distributed Systems**

5.  **Communication Protocols**
    
    - **REST vs. gRPC vs. WebSockets**: Choosing protocols based on latency, payload size, and use case .
    - **Message Queues**: Kafka, RabbitMQ for async processing and decoupling services .
6.  **Fault Tolerance & Redundancy**
    
    - **Failover Mechanisms**: Active-passive vs. active-active configurations .
    - **Circuit Breakers**: Preventing cascading failures (e.g., Hystrix, Resilience4j) .
    - **Idempotency & Retries**: Ensuring operations are repeatable without side effects .

* * *

### **4\. Advanced Topics**

7.  **System Bottlenecks**
    
    - **Back-of-the-Envelope Calculations**: Estimating storage, bandwidth, and QPS (e.g., 1M users → 40K RPS) .
    - **Bottleneck Identification**: Profiling CPU, memory, I/O, and network usage .
8.  **Cloud & DevOps**
    
    - **Auto-Scaling**: AWS Autoscaling, Kubernetes for dynamic resource allocation .
    - **Infrastructure as Code (IaC)**: Terraform, CloudFormation for repeatable deployments .
    - **Monitoring & Logging**: Tools like CloudWatch, Prometheus, and ELK stack .

* * *

### **5\. Real-World Patterns**

9.  **Design Patterns**
    
    - **Microservices vs. Monoliths**: Trade-offs in modularity, scalability, and complexity .
    - **Event Sourcing & CQRS**: Managing state changes and read/write separation .
10. **Case Studies**
    
    - **Twitter/Instagram Feeds**: Fan-out, timeline merging, and caching .
    - **URL Shorteners**: Hash collision resolution, distributed key-value stores .
